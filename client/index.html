About my services

I build websites, web applications, mobile apps, data processing pipelines, and anything else you need, and I build them in the most performance-optimal and cost effective way possible.

I am a certified AWS developer and I am well versed in AWS, its managed services, and cloud architecture approaches. I stay up to date on all the cutting edge cloud technologies and I leverage this expertise in every app I build to ensure that it is not only the best experience for your customers, but also the best experience for your wallet.

To ensure quality, I generally take a serverless-first approach to application architecture. I build most of my applications using Node JS, although I also have experience with Python and Java on the back end, and I am open to working with other back end languages if the project necessitates it. One of the many benefits of serverless is that it is language agnostic, and future features can be developed in almost any back end language to meet future feature or developer needs. React.js is my Javascript framework of choice, but I am always happy to use another framework if the project requires it.
